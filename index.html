About

I study how uncertainty and expectations shape perception, pain, and social judgment. My work combines behavioural experiments, multi-echo model-based fMRI, and computational frameworks (predictive processing, information-theoretic variables, DCM).

Keywords: predictive processing · uncertainty & surprise · pain valuation · effective connectivity · model-based fMRI
