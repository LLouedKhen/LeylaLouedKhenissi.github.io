About

I work on consciousness, predictive coding, and decision-making. My research combines computational modeling, multi-echo fMRI, and behavioral experiments to understand how the brain generates predictions about the world, the self, and others.

Keywords: consciousness · predictive processing · self/other inference · neuroeconomics · decision-making · computational modeling
